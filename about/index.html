I have a very firm belief, that a person’s life is worth only as much as their impact on others. Through photography, I intend to tell stories that need to be told. The idea of capturing a brilliant moment in its most raw form, or a mundane occurrence in truly extraordinary light deeply excites me.

I’m 19 years old and am currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science. Like photography, it’s something very dear to me. I have been extremely fortunate to have a family that has given me the privilege of pursuing my passions, and I intend to never stop.

If you would like to talk about work, photography, or life in general, please feel free to do so by writing to me at photos@raghavthakar.com or messaging me on Instagram or Twitter.
